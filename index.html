About

Favorite Pokemon is a free online tool that helps you discover your true #1 favorite Pokemon through tournament-style elimination brackets. All 1,025 Pokemon from Gen 1 (Kanto) through Gen 9 (Paldea) are included.

Features

  • Tournament bracket elimination
  • Taste Profile with uniqueness score
  • Hot Takes detection (upset victories)
  • Type and generation preference analysis
  • Quick mode (6 Pokemon) and Standard mode (11 Pokemon)
  • No login required
